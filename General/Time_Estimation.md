> [!tip] Time Estimation Considerations.
>
> - **Technical side:** and here it depends whether i know how exactly the task can be implemented or not.
> > - If i do not know how to implement the task, i have to add time for research to know how to implement it.
> > - If i know how to implement the task, i can estimate the time based on my experience.
> - After implementaion estimation add a margin for unexpected issues.
> - **Data quality and completeness:** Assess the quality and completeness of the data sources involved in the task, this is a major bottlleneck that can significantly affect the time estimation.
> - **Resource availability(Remote Machine Issue):** Consider the availability of resources, including personnel and tools, that may impact the task timeline, for example the remote machine slowness and more than one person working on the same machine during the day that affects the time estimation.
> - **Dependencies:** Identify any dependencies on other tasks or processes that may affect the timeline.
> - **Reading emails and Meetings:** Include time for reading emails and attending meetings related to the task, as these can take up a significant portion of the workday.
> - **Testing and validation requirements:** Account for the time needed for testing and validating the results of the task.
